Fried rice

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • eggs
  • Rice
  • meat
  • vegetables

Directions

  1. The rice should be kept in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 hours
  2. Add a little vegetable oil to the rice before frying mix well
  3. Rice with oil and egg mixture under the hot pot
  4. Then add salt and chopped green onion, stir fry
